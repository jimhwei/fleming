About Me

I am a Junior Environmental Geoscientist (GIT) with 3 year of experience in field work, GPS surveying, GIS mapping and environmental reporting in a consulting environment. My experience includes working with GIS, Python, SQL Relational Databases, and AutoCAD drafting in a project-oriented setting. I graduated from University of Toronto Environmental Geoscience Specialist program with a focus on Geology and GIS.

When I am not busy, I like to make food, learn new languages, lift weights and explore new places.
