About Me

I am a Junior Environmental Geoscientist (GIT) with 3 year of experience in field work, GPS surveying, GIS mapping and environmental reporting. I have produced technical drawings for environmental assessment projects via research, digitization, and spatial data annotation. My experience includes using GIS, Python, SQL, and AutoCAD in a project-oriented setting. Graduate from University of Toronto Environmental Geoscience Specialist program with a focus on Geology and GIS.

When I am not busy, I like to make food, lift weights and explore new places.

I also like dogs and playing video games.
